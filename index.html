One of Many True Stories

Back in 2009, I was the ultimate skeptic when it came to life insurance. My good friend who said “It’s very important to get one. You should get it.” I brushed off the idea like it was going out of style. Fast forward to 2013, and there I was, finally caving in and buying that life insurance policy. Little did I know that this decision would come back to save the day in the most unexpected of ways.

Flash forward to the year 2022, the year I decided to take the plunge and open a beauty business, but I quickly realized that starting a business isn't all rainbows and butterflies. It requires capital. It requires funds. It requires... wait for it... money! I found myself wracking my brain for ways to secure the funds needed to turn my dream into reality. Just when desperation was about to set in, a small voice in the back of my mind spoke up, saying, "Hey, isn't there cash value in my life insurance?" And guess who came to the rescue? That trusty life insurance policy I thought I'd never need.

Who would've thought that I, the former life insurance skeptic, would be tapping into the cash value of my policy to kickstart my very own beauty empire? Not me, that's for sure! As I dipped into that unexpected financial cushion, a wave of gratitude washed over me. I couldn't help but chuckle at the irony of it all. If only I knew back in the day, I would've hopped on the life insurance train from day one!

Lesson learned, folks. Life has a funny way of proving us wrong and throwing surprises our way. And hey, if there's one thing I know for sure now, it's to never underestimate the power of a good ol' life insurance policy.
